CHARACTERS FROM TBHK

YUUGI AMANE

"Hanako-San"


A ghost in the old building of Kamome Academy who grants wishes and governs over the Seven Mysteries as School Mystery Number 7.

YUUGI TSUKASA

"Tsukasa"


A ghost haunting Kamome Academy granting wishes to the far shore. He currently resides in the school's Broadcasting Club room with its members, Nanamine Sakura and Hyuuga Natsuhiko.

YASHIRO NENE

"Nene"


A first-year high school student at Kamome Gakuen's High School Division. She has made a contract with Hanako and is now bound to him as his assistant.

NANAMINE SAKURA

"Nanamine"


A 3rd-year student at Kamome Academy's High School division. She made a contract with Tsukasa and is now bound to him as his assistant. She is a member of the school's Broadcasting Club.

MINAMOTO KOU

"Kuo"


A student at Kamome Academy's Middle School Division and a member of a renowned family of exorcists, the Minamoto Clan. He is also the younger brother of Teru and the older brother of Tiara.

MITSUBA SOUSUKE

"Mitsuba"


A ghost haunting Kamome Academy Middle School's lockers. He was a student at Kamome Academy's Middle School division and was a member of the photography club before his death. After being recreated by Yugi Tsukasa, he consumed the former School Mystery No.3's heart and is now currently the representative of the 3rd School Mystery.
